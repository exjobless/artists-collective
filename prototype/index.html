Open an account,
own a piece of the gallery.

You don't buy in. When you join, the network gives you a stake — for free. Ownership comes from taking part. No keys or wallets to learn: you sign up, and you're in.

1
An on-chain identity, instantly. A ledger address is created for you the moment you sign up — recorded on Usernode and validated by the nodes.
2
A free founding stake. Starter tokens land in your account. Artists hold the majority, so artists steer the gallery.
3
You set the fee. The cut — and where it goes — is voted on by the artists together, not set by a company.

The on-chain ledger

Every artist who joins and every sale is written to Usernode as a permanent, tamper-proof record — who, what, how much, and the fee split. The money moves in fiat; the chain keeps the truth. Buyers never see this — it's the artists' shared book.
